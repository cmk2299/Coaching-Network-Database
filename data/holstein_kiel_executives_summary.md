#### **Finn Jaensch**
- **Role:** Sportlicher Leiter (U17-U23) - Sporting Director Youth
- **Period:** Summer 2018 - December 2024 (6.5 years)
- **Total Tenure at Club:** 15 years (2010-2024)
- **Key Facts:**
  - Started as U12 coach in 2010
  - Progressed through U13, U17, U19 coaching roles
  - Promoted to Sportlicher Leiter in summer 2018
  - Responsible for sporting direction of U17-U23 age groups
  - Left for SV Werder Bremen (Sporting Director U15-U17) in December 2024
- **Sources:**
  - [Personelle Veränderungen im Nachwuchsleistungszentrum](https://www.holstein-kiel.de/jungstoerche/personelle-veraenderungen-im-nachwuchsleistungszentrum/)

### 2. SCOUTING DIRECTORS

#### **Frank Spitzer**
- **Role:** Leiter Scouting NLZ (Head of Youth Scouting)
- **Period:** 2018 - December 2024 (6 years)
- **Key Facts:**
  - Head of scouting for youth academy for 6 years
  - Responsible for squad planning for U23
  - Previously worked in scouting at St. Pauli
  - Left to become Sporting Director at FC Eintracht Norderstedt (December 1, 2024)
  - Replaced by Christian Klose (April 2025)
- **Sources:**
  - [Kicker: Vertragsauflösung bei Holstein Kiel: Spitzer wird Sportlicher Leiter in Norderstedt](https://www.kicker.de/vertragsaufloesung-bei-holstein-kiel-spitzer-wird-sportlicher-leiter-in-norderstedt-1067783/artikel)
